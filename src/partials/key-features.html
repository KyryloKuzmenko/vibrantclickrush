Key Features

  • Factory Optimization

    Design and expand your bullet production facility with various machines, conveyor belts, and storage units

  • Weapon Customization

    Upgrade and modify your hero's weapons, balancing attributes like fire rate, damage, and magazine size

  • Automated Production

    Set up intricate production lines that continue to generate ammunition even when you're offline

  • Hero Progression

    Level up your hero and unlock new abilities and equipment to enhance combat effectiveness

  • Research & Development

    Invest in research to unlock advanced machinery, more efficient production methods, and powerful bullet types

  • Dynamic Enemy Waves

    Confront diverse enemy types and escalating challenges that require strategic adjustments to your supply chain

  • Leaderboards & Achievements

    Compete with players globally and earn achievements for mastering supply chain management and combat
