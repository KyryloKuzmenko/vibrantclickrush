Main Characters Description

  • The Engineer (Player Character)

    A brilliant logistical mind, responsible for designing, building, and optimizing the ammunition production facility. They are the strategic backbone of the operation.

  • The Hero

    A skilled and resilient combatant on the frontline, directly supported by the Engineer's supply efforts. Their effectiveness in battle depends entirely on a consistent flow of ammunition.

  • The Quartermaster

    An essential advisor who provides intelligence on enemy threats, resource needs, and potential upgrades for the supply chain.


  • The Mechanist

    A specialist in factory automation and upgrades, offering insights into new machinery and efficiency improvements for bullet production.
